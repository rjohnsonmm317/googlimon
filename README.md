# GOOGLIMON: THE INDEX CRISIS
### Generation I — Browser RPG

> *"What if information itself became a combat resource?"*

---

## What Is Googlimon?

Googlimon is an original browser-based monster-collecting RPG set in the Googlia region — a modern world built on a mysterious infrastructure called **The Index**. Strange glitches are spreading across the region. Routes disappear from maps. Googlimon evolve incorrectly. Devices reboot without warning.

You start in Cache Town with a starter Googlimon from Professor Search. What begins as a badge quest slowly becomes an investigation into the foundations of civilization itself.

---

## The World of Googlia

Googlia is a region where technology, biology, and ancient history collide. Every city, communication network, and Googlimon ecosystem is connected through The Index — a caretaker intelligence no one fully understands. Most citizens treat it as a fact of life. Very few know where it came from.

**Major Locations:**
- Cache Town — Starting town, Professor Search's Lab
- Searchburg — Gym 1 (Search type)
- Browser Bay — Gym 2 (Browser type)
- Streamport — Gym 3 (Media type)

**Postgame Areas:** Deep Web, Dark Web, Legacy Servers

## Types

14 original types built around internet and technology concepts:

`Search` `Browser` `Cloud` `Data` `Media` `AI` `Security` `Gaming` `Social` `Hardware` `Electric` `Virus` `Quantum` `Cosmic`

The type system uses three core triangles:
- **Information Triangle:** Search > Data > AI > Search
- **Internet Triangle:** Browser > Media > Social > Browser
- **Infrastructure Triangle:** Security > Virus > Hardware > Security

---

## Battle System

- 6 Googlimon teams, turn-based combat, 4 move slots
- Move categories: Physical, Digital, Utility
- Status conditions: Corrupted, Lagged, Crashed, Burned, Frozen, Disconnected
- **Indexed Advantage** — attacking a favorable type reveals opponent stats
- **Indexed Forms** — once-per-battle transformation granting +20% all stats and a signature move
- **Breakthroughs** — the critical hit system
- Weather: Rain, Storm, Outage, Network Surge, Quantum Flux

## Key Characters

**Professor Search** — Mentor, researcher, and secretly the former undefeated Champion. Investigates Index disturbances from Cache Town.

**Admin Null** — Former Chief Index Engineer. Founded Team Malware after being dismissed for claiming The Index was discovered, not built.

**Avery** — Primary rival. Professor Search's top student. Learns that responsibility matters more than optimization.

**Riley** — Team Malware member who becomes an ally. Uses Security, Virus, and Hardware types.

**Livia** — Streamport streamer and rival. Gives up her platform to reveal the truth about Nullbyte.

**Nullbyte** — The primary antagonist. Embodies deletion, corruption, and entropy. Does not seek power — it seeks absence.
